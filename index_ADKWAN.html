MEAD.COM – Five Boxes + Fixed Sidebar

1) BRAND: The brand is very “educational” in the sense that it appeals to an audience that cares about academia. This is represented by the very neat, cursive writing in the navigation header. 2) FUNCTIONALITY: This site is functional for its purpose, which is to appeal to consumers in the education industry. The tabs at the top and directly below the main object box provide enough information for efficient navigation throughout the site. 3) INTERACTIVITY: The interactivity on the site is decent, as mouse over highlights are implemented along the header and sidebar. This allows for efficient navigation. 4) NAVIGATION: Most of the navigation is found among the highlights, but this is sufficient in creating easy navigation and keeping the site from becoming too interactive for some users in the education demographic. SCHOLASTIC.COM/HOME – Advanced Grid

1) BRAND: The brand is very “neat” and “clean.” It appeals to an audience that cares about organization and order. 2) FUNCTIONALITY: This site does a good job of showing relevant information to the proposed audience. As a company whose products are based around reading for educational purposes, the various interactive box act supplementary to the navigation toolbar at the top. 3) INTERACTIVITY: There is limited interactivity on the main home page of the site, but it has enough to let the user navigate easily. 4) NAVIGATION: The navigation in this site focuses only on what is necessary for immediate knowledge of particular details, so users are able to navigate simply, yet effectively.

MITCHELLKPHOTOS.COM – Featured Photo 1) BRAND: The brand is very “professional” and “sophisticated.” As a professional photographer’s website, it appeals to clients who are looking for a reliable and experienced photographer to do work for them. 2) FUNCTIONALITY: This site does an excellent job of portraying photography as the main focus. With the featured photo as a slideshow, the site allows the photographer to exhibit some of his best work. The navigation toolbar at the top contain links to more relevant information about the photographer and his work. 3) INTERACTIVITY: The main source of interactivity is in the slideshow of photos, which does a beautiful job of letting the user “feel into” the photo as he/she clicks through them. The highlights in the navigation bar are especially useful for navigation. 4) NAVIGATION: As a photograph exhibition website, the navigation is geared towards information about the photographer, and this site does a focused and beautiful job in promoting his work.

SONY.COM – Featured Graphic + Five Boxes 1) BRAND: The brand is very “clean” as a technology website. It appeals to an audience who likes pure, simple beauty of products. 2) FUNCTIONALITY: This site does an excellent job of exhibiting their newest, appealing product in the featured photo section. In the lower half of the homepage, further product details of the company are clearly shown, and also display newer products. 3) INTERACTIVITY: The interactivity on this site is excellent, especially within the navigation bar. Pop-down menus and highlights during mouse over effectively let the user navigate the site easily. 4) NAVIGATION: The navigation in this site is very good. Since there are multiple branches in the company, the smooth navigation toolbar is very effective in creating an almost flawless journey throughout the many crevices of the site.

U2.COM – Power Grid 1) BRAND: The brand is “edgy”, as best represented by the choice in font. Using a bolded, all-caps font, the brand is presented as very modern. 2) FUNCTIONALITY: As a band web page, this site does a good job of taking advantage of the power grid design to allow users access to multiple facets of the website, while mutually advertising the band. 3) INTERACTIVITY: With the power grid design, there is a lot of interactivity, especially among the video and audio aspects of the site. And because the site is promoting a band, this is especially beneficial to their promotion. 4) NAVIGATION: At first, the navigation may seem a little bit cluttered, but in fact, it is not, because the top navigation toolbar does a sufficient job in leading to relevant information about the band, while the extra object boxes provide direct and immediate access to popular aspects.
